Guessing Game
Welcome to the Guessing Game! Here's how it works: I'm thinking of a number. Try to guess it. You have 60 seconds. I'll help you visualize your progress and indicate whether you are moving in the right or wrong direction (toward the center or away from it). Numbers on the left and right represent the lower and upper bounds on the search domain. Thats it. When your ready, press enter...
